The Best Show/Animes According To James

My Top Animes of all-time

Detective Conan

A child in form, yet sharp in mind,
Unraveling truths the world can’t find.
With every case, he plays his part,
A tiny sleuth with a giant heart.

Naruto Shippuden

Through pain and loss, he walks alone,
Yet bonds of fire and will have grown.
With dreams of peace, he stands so tall,
A ninja sworn to save them all.

One Piece

Across the seas, through storms untamed,
A pirate’s dream, his heart inflamed.
With crew and will, he sails so free,
To chase the dawn and destiny.

My Top Show of all-time

Young Sheldon

A boy so bright, yet misunderstood,
In Texas town where rules still stood.
With science sharp and questions grand,
He walks a path none planned or planned.

Sherlock Holmes

Through fog and whispers, truths unfold,
A mind too sharp, both keen and cold.
No lie survives his piercing gaze,
Dark deeds dissolve in logic’s blaze.

Brooklyn Nine-Nine

Detectives sharp with jokes to spare,
Chasing crime with style and flair.
In chaos, teamwork lights the way,
Cool, cool, cool—no doubt, no way!
